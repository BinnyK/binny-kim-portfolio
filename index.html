DevMentor connects junior devs with tech leaders through the Twitter API. It provides answers to the most popular questions asked by beginning developers such as "what is the most important soft skill that a beginning dev should have?", "what would do differently if you were beginning your tech career?" and "how do I know when I'm ready to get a job as a dev?"
